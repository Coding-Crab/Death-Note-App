1. The human whose name is written in this note shall die.
  2. This note will not take effect unless the writer has the person's face in their mind when writing his/her name. Therefore, people sharing the same name will not be affected.
  3. If the cause of death is written within 40 seconds of writing the person's name, it will happen.
  4. If the cause of death is not specified, the person will simply die of a heart attack.
  5. After writing the cause of death, details of the death should be written in the next 6 minutes and 40 seconds.
  6. The Death Note will not take effect if: The person writing the name is not the original owner of the Death Note. The name is written by a person with the intention of saving the person. The name is written by someone who is unable to see the person's face.
  7. The person possessing the Death Note can extend their own life by writing their own name in the note along with the method of prolonging their life.
  8. If a Death Note is lost or destroyed, the person whose name was written in it will not die.
  9. If multiple Death Notes exist and a name is written in two or more of them, the death will occur according to the first Death Note in which the name was written.
  10. If a person other than the original owner of the Death Note touches it, that person will be unable to see the names of those who will die of a heart attack, unless the original owner writes the person's name in the Death Note.
  11. If a Death Note is left unused for more than 13 days, it will become useless.
  12. If a person uses a Death Note, their lifespan will be shortened by the number of people whose names they have written in the note.
  13. If a person possesses a Death Note, they are able to see a Shinigami (Death God), but only the owner of the Death Note can talk to the Shinigami.
